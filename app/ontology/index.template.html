This tool can generate trees from Wikidata structures, such as subclasses, administrative divisions, and taxons.

Examples:

  • Places in Cambridgeshire
  • All birds (takes a few seconds)
  • Location subclasses
  • Parts of World War II (quite sparse...)

Options:

  • For small result sets (less than ~250 items), try the d3 "star tree"
  • Add "&lang=de" to get German labels, if available (works for all languages)
